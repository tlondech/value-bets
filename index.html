How to read these signals.

Read this once to get the most out of the data.

  • 1

    Variance is real.

    Not every signal will land. Our models identify a statistical edge (+EV), but variance happens. The edge shows up over hundreds of signals, not just one weekend.

  • 2

    Stake responsibly.

    If you act on a signal, never allocate more than 1–2% of your total bankroll to a single signal. Staking discipline matters as much as the model.

  • 3

    Market odds adjust quickly.

    Bookmakers reprice mispriced odds fast. High +EV signals carry the most edge at the moment they appear.
