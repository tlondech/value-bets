Surface-adjusted Elo ratings from the last 5 seasons of ATP/WTA data. Win probability blends overall Elo (40%) with surface-specific Elo (60%) for Hard, Clay, and Grass courts.

Card Indicators

Recent form — last 5 games (green W, red L), oldest on the left. No draws in basketball.
⏱ 1d Back-to-back — team played the night before. The model reduces their expected scoring by 2.5 pts.

Model

Gaussian efficiency model using rolling team game logs from the current NBA season. Expected scores are derived from each team's home/away attack and defensive ratings, adjusted for home-court advantage (+3.5 pts) and back-to-back fatigue (−2.5 pts). A Normal distribution over the point differential and total drives all three market probabilities.

EV Colour Coding

+8.5% Normal edge — good value bet.
+14.2% High edge (≥10%) — strong value, verify the odds are still available.
+23.1% ⚠ Very high edge (≥20%) — unusually wide; double-check the line before betting.
